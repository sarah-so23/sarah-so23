WILL I RECEIVE AN EMAIL CONFIRMATION WITH MY ORDER?

Yes! You will receive an email confirmation with your order summary.

HOW CAN I TRACK MY ORDER?

Yes, once we ship your order, you will receive a confirmation email that your order has been shipped. In that email, there will be a tracking number.

CARRIERS WE WORK WITH:

  • FedEX
  • UPS
  • Canada Post (For Canada-based orders only)
  • USPS (For US-based orders only)

CAN I PICK UP MY ORDER?

Unfortunately, we do not have a pickup order option in place.

CAN I CANCEL MY ORDER?

We can cancel your order as long as it has not been fulfilled. Please email us your Order Number at Pearly_Crochet@gmail.com to cancel an existing order. We typically fulfill orders 1-2 days after the order is made. After an order is fulfilled, it is en route and can no longer be canceled. For returns, please see below.

CAN I RETURN MY ORDER?

We do accept returns as long as none of the items are damaged, such as soiled, ripped stitches, etc.
